Facts about cat

1.Unique nose prints: Like human fingerprints, every cat's nose pad has a unique pattern of ridges and bumps.

2.Extra bones: A cat has more than 230 bones in its body, compared to a human's 206. Their extra vertebrae give them great flexibility.

3.Super hearing: Cats can hear sounds at a much higher frequency than both humans and dogs, allowing them to pinpoint the location of small prey like rodents.

4.Third eyelid: Cats have a "nictitating membrane" that acts as a third eyelid, providing extra protection and moisture for their eyes.

5.No sweet tooth: Due to a genetic mutation, cats cannot taste sweet flavors.
